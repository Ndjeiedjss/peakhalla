Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026. This page explains how PeakHalla handles website usage data, accounts, cookies, and advertising services.

Information PeakHalla processes

PeakHalla may process public Brawlhalla player identifiers and statistics to provide profiles, rankings, clans, teammates, Queue activity estimates, and tournament information. Arena Wall accounts may store a username, a securely processed password, optional profile details, posts, comments, and uploaded images.

Technical data

The hosting platform and security systems may receive technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, requested pages, timestamps, and error logs. This information is used to operate, secure, diagnose, and improve the service.

Cookies and local storage

PeakHalla uses browser storage for preferences such as theme, language, recent cached statistics, and account sessions. These technologies help the website load faster and remember settings.

Advertising

PeakHalla may display direct sponsorships or third-party advertising. If Google advertising is activated, third-party vendors including Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor’s prior visits to this and other websites. Google’s advertising cookies enable Google and its partners to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on user choices and applicable law.

Users may manage personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings. PeakHalla may use a consent management message for visitors in regions where consent is required.

Third-party links and services

The website links to services such as Discord, PayPal, tournament platforms, social networks, and data providers. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data retention and removal

Operational records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for the service, security, legal compliance, or legitimate business purposes. To request removal of an Arena Wall account or content, contact the PeakHalla team through Discord.

Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the PeakHalla Discord server.
