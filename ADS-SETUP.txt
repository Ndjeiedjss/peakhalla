PEAKHALLA ADS SETUP — v7.76

CURRENT STATE
- Direct sponsorship placeholders are enabled.
- They send interested advertisers to the PeakHalla Discord.
- Google AdSense is prepared but disabled until you add your real IDs.

TO ACTIVATE ADSENSE
1. Apply at https://adsense.google.com/ and add peakhalla.com.
2. In public/ads-config.js:
   - Change provider: 'sponsor' to provider: 'adsense'
   - Add your client ID, for example ca-pub-1234567890123456
   - Add the numeric ad slot ID for each placement.
3. Create public/ads.txt with the exact line Google gives you, usually:
   google.com, pub-1234567890123456, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0
4. In AdSense > Privacy & messaging, configure Google's consent message for the EEA, UK, and Switzerland.
5. Deploy again.

DIRECT SPONSOR ADS
- Edit public/ads-config.js.
- Change the title, description, CTA, URL, and image per placement.
- An image may be a public URL such as /assets/sponsors/example.webp.
