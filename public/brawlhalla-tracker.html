Why use PeakHalla?

PeakHalla is designed for players who want one place to search a profile, check current and peak ELO, inspect legend performance, find old names, view ranked teammates, follow clans, compare regional ladders and discover tournaments.

Supported ranked modes and regions

The tracker supports 1v1, 2v2 and 3v3 views where official data is available. Regional pages cover All Regions, Middle East, Europe, US East, US West, Southern Africa, Southeast Asia, Brazil, Australia and Japan.

Fast profile loading

Previously viewed profiles are prepared from PeakHalla’s saved database immediately, then checked against current official data in the background. This keeps profile pages useful even during temporary upstream API issues.
