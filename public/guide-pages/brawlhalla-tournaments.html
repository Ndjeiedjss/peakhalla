Brawlhalla Tournament Calendar and Prize Pools

Use PeakHalla to find live, upcoming and completed Brawlhalla events by region and mode.

Overview

The tournament directory separates official and community events and shows region, 1v1 or 2v2 mode, dates, status and prize pool when available.

Event status

Live events appear first, followed by upcoming, announced and recently completed tournaments.

Registration

Open the source link on each tournament card for official rules, eligibility and registration details.

Use it on PeakHalla

Search a player or open the matching ranked leaderboard to see the information in a live profile. PeakHalla keeps saved data ready for fast loading and checks current sources in the background.
